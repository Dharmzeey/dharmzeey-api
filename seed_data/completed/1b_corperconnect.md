# CorperConnect

**CorperConnect** is a social web platform created to help corps members and young professionals share their service experiences, ideas, and stories in one connected space.  
It blends the power of content sharing with a growing online community.

The backend is built with **Django** and **Django Rest Framework (DRF)** for robust data handling and authentication, while the frontend uses **Next.js** for fast rendering and seamless interaction.

---

## Features

### Home & Feed
![Feed Page](/images/corperconnect/feed.jpg)  
A dynamic feed that displays trending posts, discussions, and shared experiences from users across different regions.

---

## User Profiles
Each user has a customizable profile where they can share their bio, posts, and service experiences, making connection easy and personal.

### **User Features:**
- Create, edit, and manage personal profiles  
- Post updates, images, or reflections  
- Engage with others through likes, comments, and reposts  
- Follow other users to build meaningful networks  
- Access trending and featured posts

---

## Admin Panel
Admins can moderate posts, manage users, and ensure smooth platform operation.

### **Admin Features:**
- Review and manage user-generated content  
- Monitor activity for compliance and engagement metrics
